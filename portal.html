The genre tiles are stylized previews — the same captured motion re-shaped by each genre's motion signature (energy, verticality, arm-lead, expansiveness, bounce, turn). They are a heuristic proxy, not the identity-preserving zone-α blend, which runs in the local pipeline. “Original” is your true captured motion; “⬇ JSON” exports it (HumanML3D-22, y-up).
