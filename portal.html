Runs entirely in your browser (nothing is uploaded). Pose via MediaPipe Tasks Vision (CDN). First principle: protect the identity movement. When the AIST genre references are available, each tile runs the real per-zone transfer (a browser port of pipeline/genre_style.py): content = your motion, style = a genre reference clip, rhythm-aligned and blended per anatomical zone by the α slider (0 = you, 1 = full genre). Root is protected; limbs carry the genre. If the references aren't loaded it falls back to heuristic editions. Note the α-trap: high α smooths energy away - 0.3-0.5 reads best. “Original” is untouched; “⬇ JSON” exports it (HumanML3D-22, y-up), which feeds run_local.py.
